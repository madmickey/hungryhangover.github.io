Brine chicken 1l per kg approx. 1/8 cup table salt and 1/8 cup sugar (be meagre) per litre. So 6 chicken maryland 400g*6=2.5 litres, 1/3 cup ish. Post brine:
Liberally sprinkle salt on 6 chicken maryland, and rest at room temp 45 min.
Cook 1/2kg potatoes for 15m, halve, cool
Cook 1 tbsp. oil, the 2 lemons sliced 10mm thick, salt, and pepper 4 mins til charred.
Add Lemons, 200gs morels or mushrooms, potatoes, 500g asparagus, 1 tsp fenugreek seeds, and chicken skin side up cook 45 mins @180, @70C is done inside.
