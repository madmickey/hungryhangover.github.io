Chuck ribs
heat pot, salt and peppar ribs, brown, and remove from pan.
slowly sauté garlic (4), onion(1), carrot(2) and celery(2)
add tomato paste (to thicken the sauce + a touch of sour), beef broth/stock(2 cups), red wine(2 cups), thyme (optional) and bay leaves (essential). worcestershire sauce,
oven – 3 hours at 160C, beef should be submerged.
