osso bucco (6)
Flour ~2kg osso bucco, and brown seperately (in the dutch oven or pot you're going to use)
Add 2 carrots (chopped), 2 onions (chopped), 2 garlic, 2 anchovy fillets, add 1/3 bottle wine, and cook for a bit.
Add tomatoes (1 can), 3 cups stock, thyme, bay leaf, bring to boil.
Cook 2-4 hours
Sprinkle gremolata (1 lemon zested, 1 orange zested, 2 cloves garlic minced, chopped parsley) to serve with potatoes
