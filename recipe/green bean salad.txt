green bean salad

https://cookieandkate.com/green-bean-salad-recipe/

toast 1/3 cup almonds, remove and add green beans and salt + water, cook 7 mins
whisk together olive oil, dijon, 1 sm garlic, 1/2 lemon, peppar.
When bean cooked, pour over with 1/8 cup crumbled cheese/ feta
Toss and add 1/8 cup more feta + basil
