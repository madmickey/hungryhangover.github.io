Goat curry
Squeeze half lemon over 1kg goat with bones, massage and then wash off with water
Make spice (5 grams curry powder, 2 grams Jamacian seasoning, 1g cloves, 1g pimento/allspice, .2 g black peppar)
Add 1/2 bunch spring onions(chopped), sprig thyme, 3 cloves garlic (chopped), spices  to meat and rub in for 5 mins
Cover and leave to marinate 2-24 hours


Brown goat and remove then fry onion and green onions, return goat and top up with water/ can of coconut milk and simmer 2 hours. 
20 mins before add stock and potatoes/sweet potato. And or okra 10 min before. Add a whole bird's eye chilli (unbroken).
