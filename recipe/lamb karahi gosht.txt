500g|2 -3 lbs lamb shoulder, round. cut into 2inch piece (don't discard bones they add flavor to the curry)
1	|2 red onions, chopped (Large)
1 	|1 tomatoes, chopped (Medium)
4	|6 garlic cloves, chopped
2	|4 teaspoons fresh ginger, grated
1	|1 cinnamon stick, 2-inch
1 	|teaspoon cumin seed
6	|12 green cardamoms, pods. (seeds only Discard shell.)
1/4	|1⁄2 teaspoon turmeric powder
1	|1 teaspoon garam masala
toch|1⁄8 teaspoon ground nutmeg
2	|3 bay leaves
1/8c|p1⁄4 cup chopped cilantro
2	|4 cloves, broken into pieces
1/2	|1 teaspoon indian curry powder
1⁄2 teaspoon salt (Or to your taste)
3	|4 -6 serrano chilies, chopped
1	|2 teaspoons coriander powder
vegetable oil

Marinate lamb in ginger, chilli/cayenne, garlic
oil, chopped onion, cumin seeds, bay leaf, cinnamon, cloves 8 min til golden
add lamb 8 min + a tbsp water
add garam masala, chillis, curry powder, salt, nutmeg, turmeric powder, ground coriander, 10 min. 
stir, 10 min
add tomatoes + water if gravy
cook 45 mins medium heat
add cilantro, optional boiled eggs
