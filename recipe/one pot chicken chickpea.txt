Onepot chicken chickpea dish

Brown (750g=4 boneless skinless chicken thighs) chicken, remove.
Add 1 onion chopped, 1 bell peppar chopped, 2 mins, add 4 cloves garlic minced 30 sec.
Add 1 tsp salt, 1/4 tsp peppar, 1sp smoked paprika, 1/2 tsp cumin, 1 can diced tomatoes, 400ml broth, stir in 1 cup long grain rice (rinsed, drained), chicken and simmer for 35 mins. 
Garnish with parsley.
