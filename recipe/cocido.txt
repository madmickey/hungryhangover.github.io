cocido (for 3)

pinch saffron in 1.5l water
add 250g chickpeas that have been soaked overnight, half chicken cut in 6, 150g chorizo skinned in big chunks, 2 pork belly chops in chunks, 3 garlic, 3 rosemary. salt peppar, 180 for 4-6 hours

cook 125g stellini or midolinne pasta to eat with a good ladel of the broth + parsley finely chopped w Manchego

alt - bay leaf, leaks? ham hock, thighs.

100g dried chickpeas=250g already soaked
