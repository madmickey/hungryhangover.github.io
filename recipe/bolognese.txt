Slow cooked bolognese inspired by Heston

Caramelise 4 onions  (20 mins?)
Add 2 cans tomatoes (if poss just juice/passata), 4 cloves garlic, 1 carrots(diced) 6 celery stalks(diced). 4tsp Worcestershire, 10 drop tabasco, 10 drops fish sauce, 60ml sherry vinegar
Simultaneously fry off meat in batches, and at the end chuck in wine (1/2 bottle) to get all the goodness.Cook for 2 hours.

Eat
