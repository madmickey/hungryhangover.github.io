Pork Belly
1 kg pork belly prick the skin many times. Flip then rub in 1 tbsp cooking wine, 1 tbsp vinegar, 1 tsp pepper (white pref), tsp 5 spice, 1/2 tsp salt. Flip back, dry skin cover and marinate 12 hours.
Preheat oven to 180 , make a tinfoil dish co my inf above the fat level, leaving the fat exposed, brush with vinegar, cover with 200g rock salt. Roast 60 mins.
Remove from oven, remove salt, brush dry and grill under medium heat, 25cm from top for 20 mins.
Serve in 1x1 cm slices with mustard and fresh green veg
