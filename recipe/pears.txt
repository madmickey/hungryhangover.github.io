stewed pears

https://www.abakingjourney.com/easy-stewed-pears/

peel but leave tops, slice bottom and core out from bottom., 
oven to 180C.
to pot, add 1 bottle fruity wine, 125 g granulated sugar, 2 cloves, 1 cinnamon, 1 strip lemon zest, 1 vanilla pod opt -> to boil stir a little for sugar
Bake 45 mins with pears .. the liquid should cover them all.
Reduce sauce by half (alt leave in fridge in sauce and reduce later)
