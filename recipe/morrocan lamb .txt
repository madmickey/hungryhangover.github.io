Morrocan lamb
306 heat oil and saute 1 onion, 3 carrots and 6 potatoes chopoped. add 1.5 kg lamb (boned leg or shoulder)
Add 1 bay leaf, 1/2 cup dried apricots, cinnamon stick, ginger, 1.5 tsp allspice, 1.5 tsp ras el hanout
add 6 plum tomatoes, broth (2.5 cups). cook for 1.5 hours at x. Add chickpeas, cook for 30 more.
Add more liquid for reheat in oven.

165 - 4 hours
