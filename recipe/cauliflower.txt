Roasted cauliflower
Add a few dollops of oil and s+p to a cauliflower with bottom trimmed flat and roast for 45 min at 200C.
Add more oil and lemon juice for 15 min.
Add 1/4 of sauce below and cook for 15 min.

Top with 1/2 more sauce, and 1/4 cup toasted pine nuts/ slivered almonds, serve with salad and sauce.


Sauce:
yogurt/coyo, olive oil 1 tbsp, lemon juice 2 tbsp, garlic 1, cumin 1/4tsp ground, coriander 1/4tsp ground, turmeric 1/4tsp, 1/4tsp ground maple syrup, salt, and pnch cayenne.
